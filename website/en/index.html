Preface

Before the journey begins

What if you held in your hands irrefutable proof that the universe must have a Creator? No gut feeling, no guesses and no doubts. A logically compelling conclusion.

This website guides you step by step through a logical chain of reasoning that shows:

  • The universe cannot have created itself.
  • It cannot exist eternally.
  • And it cannot have arisen from nothing.

The only logical conclusion for the universe and the origin of life is an almighty Creator. Neither chance nor a mindless force can be responsible for it.

The chain of reasoning rests on necessary truths of logic. These hold independently of human or machine progress. For a logical impossibility remains impossible forever, just as 2 + 2 will never equal 5.
